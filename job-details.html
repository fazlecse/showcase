Senior UX/UI Designer India location - all levels
Centime —Anywhere

Are you a Senior UX/UI designer with exceptional design skills who is looking for the right home — one where you can engage in innovative, exciting work? Is it your dream to collaborate with some of the world's best creative teams?


We offer an opportunity for professional growth for people who aspire to work in a challenging, well-rounded environment where multibillion-dollar problems are solved. It is a unique role in which you can create high-level design work on a global scale. You will also gain an experience at par with some of the top design firms in the world, while both listening and being heard.


Compensation Best in Industry, ESOPs, and Equity


Working days 5 days/week


What does it mean to work for Centime


You will work with some of the best talents in the business. Our design standards are very high and can match any world-class product design. You will be proud of the work you will create and learn every day. Teams with no egos. The best benefits include comprehensive health insurance, unlimited vacation time, work onsite in Hyderabad, offsite, or remotely from anywhere in India.
